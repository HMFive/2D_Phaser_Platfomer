Paper Mario Review



Paper Mario has made the bar too high for turn-based JRPG's that now every turn-based game should copy its combat, because Paper Mario made turn-based combat fun and not irritating.Instead of waiting your turn while your brain turned off, you need to pay attain to combat unlike any other turn-based JRPG. This has done by pressing a button while enemy is attacking you. You can reduce the damage you take if you press the button just as enemy hit you. While it is your turn instead of just selecting attack and waiting like other turn based games you have to press extra buttons. For example if you want your attack to make more damage you have to press attack button as soon as you attack the enemy.


Paper Mario is a game that have very good art direction.It shows that even after 20 years unlike others game this aged really well.
